About the Remedy Bed

Who we are

The REMEDY BED was created for all people who need assistance from a lying position to a standing position.

It's a comfortable bed that converts to a lift chair in seconds.

Designed for Assisted Living, Hospice or At Home Care, and Rehabilitation Care.

How it works

You are in control! Regain the ability to stand independently!

Allows for easy transfer to a wheelchair.

Perfect for home or assisted living.

Supports up to 400lbs.

Features

  • Raises straight up and down for easy care.

  • Simplifies transfer movement to a wheelchair or mobility device.

  • Assists caregiver with easy lift function to raise individual out of the bed for lavatory care.

  • Adjusts to a personalized comfort level.

  • Conforms to reclining chair comfort position and can raise and lower from that position.
